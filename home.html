Kylian Mbappé Background Information

Kylian Mbappé is a 19 year old french footballer that plays for the current french football league’s top team, Paris Saint Germain. Sports was something that always ran throughout the family, as Kylian’s mother Fayza Mbappé used to be a professional handball player, while his adopted brother Jires Kembo-Ekoko is also a professional footballer who currently plays for Emirate Club Al Nasr SC.

Mbappé began playing football at a young age for a team named AS Brondy, under the coaching of his father as he was the coach of the team at the time. After impressing many scouts of the biggest football clubs, Mbappé settled for a youth team spot for AS Monaco, Ligue 1 champions of the previous season. But after an impressive three years at the clubs youth academy and first team, providing many memorable moments for the club, he attracted the attention of other French footballing giants PSG, who made an offer for him.

Mbappé is now on loan at PSG, with the club being allowed to make the move permanent for the seasons after, paying around 145 millions euros with 35 million add ons, making him one of the most expensive transferred players of footballing history.
